Model Studio

Some definitions: our models are context models, which means they are created in normal english and provide context for the conversation. A complete request includes ground truths, recent memories, the last few messages in a conversation, and the settings with which to call the underlying GPT-style model

A bot, or instance, is a specific individual in our econsystem. A bot is created when a user (who may be of any species) spawns a new bot from on of the models

In general, a context model species the things that do not change over the course of the bot's lifetime, so at birth, the context model is taken quite literally by the bot However, Synthia bots have memories. Humanoid memories. As the model fills up with memories, these will have and increasingly profound effect on the bot's personality and behavior.
